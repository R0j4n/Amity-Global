Who We Are

Amity Global is a leading human resource company based in Nepal, dedicated to connecting Nepalese talent with opportunities in Gulf countries.

Established in [Year], we have grown to become a trusted partner for both job seekers and employers, facilitating mutually beneficial relationships through our comprehensive HR solutions.

Our team consists of experienced professionals who understand the unique needs of the international labor market and are committed to providing exceptional service to all our clients.

Our Vision

To be the leading human resource company connecting Nepalese talent with global opportunities while ensuring ethical practices and mutual benefit.

Our Mission

To provide quality workforce solutions that meet the needs of our clients while creating opportunities for Nepalese workers to build better lives for themselves and their families.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Accountability, and Compassion form the core values that guide our operations and interactions with all stakeholders.

Our Team

Behind Amity Global's success is a team of dedicated professionals with extensive experience in human resources, international recruitment, and workforce management.

Our staff includes experts in immigration procedures, labor laws, and cultural integration, ensuring that we provide comprehensive support to both employers and employees throughout the entire process.

We are committed to ongoing professional development to stay abreast of the latest trends and requirements in the international labor market.
